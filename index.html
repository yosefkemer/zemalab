Our Diagnostic Services

Comprehensive medical testing services using advanced technology and expert analysis

Hematology

Complete blood count (CBC) and comprehensive blood analysis to detect anemia, infections, and other critical conditions.

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Blood Smear Analysis
  • Coagulation Studies
  • ESR & CRP Testing

Clinical Chemistry

Detailed biochemical analysis including lipid profiles, liver function, and kidney function tests.

  • Lipid Profile
  • Liver Function Tests
  • Kidney Function Tests
  • Diabetes Monitoring

Electrolytes & Minerals

Measurement of essential electrolytes and minerals for optimal body function and metabolic health.

  • Sodium & Potassium
  • Calcium & Phosphorus
  • Magnesium & Chloride
  • Trace Elements

Cardiac Markers

Early detection of heart conditions with comprehensive cardiac enzyme testing and biomarkers.

  • Troponin I & T
  • CK-MB
  • LDH
  • BNP/NT-proBNP

Cancer Markers

Screening for important cancer indicators for early detection and monitoring treatment response.

  • PSA (Prostate)
  • CA-125 (Ovarian)
  • CEA (Colorectal)
  • AFP (Liver)

Vitamin & Hormones

Comprehensive vitamin and hormone testing to identify deficiencies and optimize health.

  • Vitamin D, B12, Folate
  • Thyroid Function
  • Sex Hormones
  • Cortisol
