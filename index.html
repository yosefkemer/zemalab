Our Diagnostic Services

Zema Diagnostic Laboratory offers a comprehensive range of medical tests and health screenings, using advanced technology and expert staff to ensure accuracy, reliability, and patient trust.

Complete Blood Count (CBC) & Hematology Tests

We perform comprehensive blood analyses to detect anemia, infections, parasitic diseases, blood cancers, and other critical conditions. Our advanced analyzers ensure precise results that guide effective treatments. Regular monitoring helps track recovery and adjust care promptly.

Lipid Profile

Assess your heart health with our detailed lipid testing, including HDL, LDL, and total cholesterol levels. Knowing your lipid profile is essential for preventing cardiovascular diseases and managing long-term wellness.

Serum Electrolytes

We measure key electrolytes like sodium (Na+), potassium (K+), calcium (Ca++), and chloride (Cl-). Balanced electrolytes are vital for nerve function, muscle contractions, and overall fluid balance.

Cardiac Markers

Early detection saves lives. Our tests include CK, CPK, and Troponin to assess heart function and catch cardiac issues before they escalate.

Cancer Markers

We provide screening for important cancer indicators such as CA-125 and ASP, offering early insights into possible malignancies and supporting timely intervention.

Vitamin Panels

Deficiencies in vitamins D, A, and E can lead to serious health complications. Our detailed panels identify these gaps so you can take corrective action.

Infection & Inflammation Markers

Testing for CRP, RF, ASO and more helps diagnose hidden infections or inflammatory conditions that might otherwise go unnoticed.

General Health Screening & Monitoring

We offer full evaluations of your health status, detecting conditions early and tracking treatment effectiveness to ensure your best possible outcome.
